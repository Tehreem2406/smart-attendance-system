# EduSync Smart Attendance System – Project-Specific Business Model

---

## 1. Value Proposition
EduSync is a **web-based unified educational management platform** for schools, colleges, and coaching centers with:
- ✅ **Session-Based Attendance**: Unique attendance codes + optional Zoom integration (no QR/face recognition)
- ✅ **Assignment & Quiz Management**: Create, submit (PDF), and track assignments with AI plagiarism checks
- ✅ **Marks & Grades**: Record and view assignment/quiz marks with item numbers
- ✅ **Fee Management**: Generate fee vouchers, track payments, and download PDF vouchers
- ✅ **Finance Module**: Salary management, fee structures, and financial reports
- ✅ **Parent Communication**: Send WhatsApp progress reports
- ✅ **Analytics & Reports**: Attendance charts, export CSV reports, and dashboards
- ✅ **User Roles**: Admin, Teacher, Student, Finance
- ✅ **Single-Tab Protection**: Prevents opening the portal on multiple browser tabs

**Benefits**:
- Saves 40–60% of administrative time
- Eliminates paper-based processes
- Provides real-time visibility for parents and teachers
- Offers data-driven insights via analytics dashboards
